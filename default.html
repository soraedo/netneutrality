what

Net Neutrality is the idea that we should be freely able to access any website or application we want, and that Internet Service Providers (ISPs) should not be able to tamper with our connections to these websites or applications in any way or form.

why

Without Net Neutrality, ISPs would be able to throttle connection speeds or even deny access altogether to competitor websites. Or they could charge an extra fee to be able to access certain applications. No Net Neutrality means they can dictate what we can or cannot consume online.

so far

We have repelled the ISPs attempts to destroy Net Neutrality. But the battle is far from over. We need to be prepared to counter them with our voices should they try again.

how

write to your senators

Let them know the importance of Net Neutrality and urge them to protect it
